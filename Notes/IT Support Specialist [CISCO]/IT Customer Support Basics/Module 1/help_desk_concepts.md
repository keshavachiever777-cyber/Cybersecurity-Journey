# Title: Help Desk Concepts
**Date**: 24/02/2026

## Concept (in my own words) 
   > A help desk is a specialized IT team that serves as the central point of contact between employees and customers seeking support.
     A help desk has technicians whose work is to communicate with customers, understand their issue and take necessary steps to resolve the issue.

## Why this exists:
                   It exists so that issues faced by customers can be resolved and a smooth experience can be maintained for customers 

## How it works (simple): 
                          Once techinicians create a ticket they assign priority to the ticket from low to high based on the severity of issue.
                          SLA's(Service Level Agreements) are present in order to set clear expectations for customers as well as support teams.
						        SLA's define the amount of time a help desk has to respond and rersolve a customer issue based on itgs priority level. 
                          Key Performance Indicators(KPI's) are present to help desk teams measure their efficiency by tracking performance over time,
						        providing valuable data that can guide improvements in service delivery.
					           AI tools are integrated to help technicians by doing basic tasks like ticket creation and categorization.
						        AI tools can also help users by providing solutions for common issues
						        and it can help technicians by assigning tickets to the right technician based on their expertise, workload and availaibility.						  

## Analogy (if any): No Analogy

## Bullet Points:
 * Support team members help users by responding and resolving their issues based on amount of time in SLA's and KPI's help keep track of the efficiency and effectiveness of the team members. 
 * AI tools help spport team by resolving small and common issues of users, creating and assigning tickets for various issues to the right techician based on expertise and availaibility.

** ## Important terms: SLA, KPI, TIcketing, AI tools
